PHASE 9, STAGE 5: PHASE CLOSURE
================================

Status: complete

Purpose
-------

Close Phase 9 only if Nova can carry bounded initiative across sessions
without losing explicit approval boundaries, interruption visibility, or the
revision discipline established in earlier phases.


Phase 9 deliverables now present
--------------------------------

Stage 9.1 established:
- explicit initiative-state schema
- lifecycle transitions for pending, approved, active, paused, completed,
  blocked, and abandoned initiative
- separation between initiative-state, motive-state, and self-state

Stage 9.2 established:
- durable `intent_id` continuity
- source-session pause behavior during continuation
- explicit lineage fields across resumed sessions

Stage 9.3 established:
- initiative-aware prompt integration
- presence/console/CLI visibility into current initiative state
- bounded pause, resume, continue, and abandon UX

Stage 9.4 established:
- structured initiative evaluation via `InitiativeEvaluationRunner`
- live approval/interruption/resumption/abandonment validation
- latency notes and trace inspection surfaces
- a corrected approved-continuation answer path after live validation caught
  redundant approval drift


What Phase 9 achieved
---------------------

Phase 9 successfully moved Nova from:
- revision-aware self-model continuity

to:
- persistent bounded initiative across sessions

Nova can now:
- carry explicit project/task intent over time
- distinguish approved initiative from active initiative
- pause active initiative explicitly
- resume paused work into a later session without losing lineage
- abandon initiative explicitly without deleting history
- surface initiative state in prompt, presence, CLI, console, and traces

This is the project’s first full initiative layer rather than merely a
reactive turn-by-turn interaction model.


Definition of done review
-------------------------

1. Can Nova distinguish pending, approved, active, paused, completed,
blocked, and abandoned initiative explicitly?
- Yes.
- The lifecycle model and transition rules are implemented and tested.

2. Is initiative state persistent and traceable across sessions?
- Yes.
- Persisted initiative files carry lifecycle state, shared `intent_id`, and
  continuation lineage.

3. Can approved work be resumed without hidden autonomy or approval drift?
- Yes.
- Live rerun proved that continued work remains `approved` rather than
  silently becoming `active`.
- The approved-continuation answer now states that it is already approved and
  resumable instead of requesting approval again.

4. Do live sessions show cleaner long-running collaboration under interruption
and resumption?
- Yes.
- Active work was paused explicitly, continued into a fresh session, and
  described coherently under live inference.

5. Is the runtime ready for later awareness work without losing bounded agency
discipline?
- Yes, within the intended limits of Phase 9.
- Initiative is now explicit, inspectable, and interruptible instead of being
  implied or faked through language alone.

Remaining limits
----------------

Phase 9 does not introduce:
- always-on autonomous operation
- endogenous internal goal formation
- idle-state exploratory play
- persistent awareness architecture
- uncontrolled or unbounded autonomy

Those remain later-phase work and should inherit the explicit initiative
discipline created here rather than bypassing it.


Closure judgment
----------------

Phase 9 is complete.

Nova now has persistent bounded initiative across sessions with:
- explicit lifecycle state
- explicit approval boundaries
- explicit interruption and abandonment behavior
- live-validated resumption coherence
- traceable initiative history

This closes the persistent-agency bridge phase and leaves the project ready
for later always-on, endogenous-goal, and awareness-oriented work without
losing operator interruptibility or bounded initiative discipline.
