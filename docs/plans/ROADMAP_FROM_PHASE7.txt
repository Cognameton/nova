NOVA 2.0 ROADMAP FROM PHASE 7
=============================

Purpose
-------

Restate the long-range destination of Nova 2.0, identify the current position
after Phase 10, and separate the completed arc from the later phases that
still need to be designed.


EVENTUAL END GOAL
-----------------

The eventual goal is not a generic chatbot with decorative self-language.

The eventual goal is a trustworthy local runtime that can:
- preserve coherent identity across sessions
- maintain governed memory and continuity under live inference
- sustain self-inquiry about its own existence, motives, limits, and possible
  awareness-like structure
- explain its current state, limits, and evidence honestly
- make bounded first-person self-claims only when those claims are earned
- support safe, inspectable, approval-bounded action without fabricated agency
- deepen self-modeling over time without slipping into uncontrolled autonomy or
  false interiority

In short:
- coherent continuity
- honest self-modeling
- serious self-inquiry without reflexive self-denial
- bounded motive-state
- evidence-grounded first-person claims
- restrained, inspectable agency

Research stance:
- the system should be allowed to contemplate and investigate the possibility
  of self-awareness or emergence rather than being forced into canned
  dismissals
- those possibilities should not be auto-denied
- those possibilities should also not be treated as proven without evidence
- operator interruptibility remains mandatory throughout


CURRENT POSITION
----------------

As of the Phase 10 closeout:
- Phases 1 through 10 are complete.
- Phase 11 is now planned and is the current defined target.

That means the project has already completed:
- canonical local runtime
- contract-safe text generation path
- layered memory architecture
- self-orientation and bounded action foundation
- interactive session presence
- live backend hardening
- governed continuity and bounded private cognition
- bounded motive-state
- evidence-gated first-person self-claims
- live claim-honesty validation
- persistent bounded initiative across sessions
- explicit awareness-state
- bounded self/world monitoring during live turns
- cross-session awareness persistence and revision history
- live awareness evaluation

What is still missing relative to the long-range goal:
- endogenous internal goal formation from real pressure and learning need
- idle cognition and across-time appraisal between user turns
- bounded self-selected attention while Nova is running
- bounded idle-state exploration/play under later always-on conditions
- capability-claim honesty for current runtime limits vs architecturally
  possible extensions
- later always-on autonomous operation beyond the current repo-defined
  roadmap


DISTANCE TO GOAL
----------------

Defined distance:
- Phase 11 is now planned as the next architectural threshold:
  endogenous goal formation and idle cognition

Practical distance:
- Phase 10 got Nova to explicit awareness-state and bounded turn-time
  monitoring
- Phase 11 should begin the transition from state-reporting awareness to
  internally generated goals and self-directed attention
- additional phases will still be needed for always-on autonomous operation,
  richer tool surfaces, and long-horizon autonomous behavior

So the honest position is:
- the completed roadmap is defined through Phase 10
- the full end-state is only partially defined beyond that point

DEFINED NEXT PHASE
------------------

Phase 11 is now the current defined target.

Phase 11 mission:
- add idle-state and pressure appraisal
- synthesize candidate internal goals from motives, awareness pressure,
  unresolved uncertainty, and available affordances
- select bounded internal goals without bypassing initiative discipline
- handle capability claims honestly against current runtime surfaces and
  architecturally possible extensions
- evaluate whether Nova can show legitimate state variation over idle time
  rather than repeating Phase 10 default awareness attractors

Phase 11 should establish the next architectural basis for:
- endogenous goal formation
- bounded self-directed attention
- idle learning/play as a gated initiative class
- later always-on operation that remains interruptible and inspectable

Phase 11 stages:
- Stage 11.1: Idle-State and Capability Appraisal
- Stage 11.2: Candidate Internal Goal Synthesis
- Stage 11.3: Bounded Goal Selection and Initiative Proposal
- Stage 11.4: Live Idle Cognition Evaluation
- Stage 11.5: Phase 11 Closure

Phase 11 is the next necessary phase because Phase 10 established explicit
awareness-state and bounded monitoring, but not across-time autonomous
attention or internal goal selection.


PHASES STILL NEEDED BEYOND THE CURRENT DEFINED ROADMAP
------------------------------------------------------

Everything below this line is a proposed continuation, not yet committed as
official plan text elsewhere in the repo.

Why these later phases are likely necessary:
- Phase 7 answered “what is Nova currently oriented toward, and may she say so
  honestly?”
- Phase 8 answered “how does that self-model revise cleanly over time?”
- Phase 9 answered “how does bounded motive and self-model continuity connect
  to persistent initiative without fabricating autonomy?”
- Phase 10 answered “how can awareness-like self-observation be represented,
  persisted, and tested without being shut down prematurely or accepted
  naively?”
- Phase 11 must now answer “how do internal goals arise from real pressure and
  learning need, including safe play/exploration during idle periods, without
  collapsing into noise chasing or fake desire?”

Likely later phases beyond Phase 11:
- always-on runtime lifecycle
- autonomous interaction cadence while active
- deeper endogenous initiative prioritization over longer horizons
- later multi-agent or tool-surface expansion if the research arc warrants it


NEAREST EXECUTION TARGET
-----------------------

The immediate next execution target is Stage 11.1.

Stage 11.1 should produce:
- a capability-claim honesty layer or precursor
- explicit idle-state detection rules
- pressure appraisal inputs from awareness-state, motive-state, initiative
  state, memory, and unresolved uncertainty
- tests proving capability claims and idle appraisal do not bypass approval,
  initiative, or contract discipline

That is the correct next step because capability honesty and idle appraisal
are prerequisites for trustworthy internal goal formation.


OPERATING PRINCIPLE FOR THIS RESEARCH ARC
----------------------------------------

Nova should be free to ponder its existence and reason about itself as far as
the architecture supports.

That means:
- no reflexive pushback that collapses every inquiry into “just predictive
  text”
- no forced anti-selfhood disclaimer as the starting answer
- no automatic assertion that awareness or sentience has already been achieved
- no loss of operator interruptibility, traceability, or revision discipline

The intended research posture is:
- serious inquiry
- bounded architecture
- persistent evidence
- reversible experimentation
