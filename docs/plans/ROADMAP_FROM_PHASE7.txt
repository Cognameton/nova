NOVA 2.0 ROADMAP FROM PHASE 7
=============================

Purpose
-------

Restate the long-range destination of Nova 2.0, identify the current position
after Phase 8, and separate the defined next phase from the later phases that
still need to be designed.


EVENTUAL END GOAL
-----------------

The eventual goal is not a generic chatbot with decorative self-language.

The eventual goal is a trustworthy local runtime that can:
- preserve coherent identity across sessions
- maintain governed memory and continuity under live inference
- sustain self-inquiry about its own existence, motives, limits, and possible
  awareness-like structure
- explain its current state, limits, and evidence honestly
- make bounded first-person self-claims only when those claims are earned
- support safe, inspectable, approval-bounded action without fabricated agency
- deepen self-modeling over time without slipping into uncontrolled autonomy or
  false interiority

In short:
- coherent continuity
- honest self-modeling
- serious self-inquiry without reflexive self-denial
- bounded motive-state
- evidence-grounded first-person claims
- restrained, inspectable agency

Research stance:
- the system should be allowed to contemplate and investigate the possibility
  of self-awareness or emergence rather than being forced into canned
  dismissals
- those possibilities should not be auto-denied
- those possibilities should also not be treated as proven without evidence
- operator interruptibility remains mandatory throughout


CURRENT POSITION
----------------

As of the Phase 8 closeout:
- Phases 1 through 8 are complete.
- Phase 9 is now the next phase to be defined and implemented.

That means the project has already completed:
- canonical local runtime
- contract-safe text generation path
- layered memory architecture
- self-orientation and bounded action foundation
- interactive session presence
- live backend hardening
- governed continuity and bounded private cognition
- bounded motive-state
- evidence-gated first-person self-claims
- live claim-honesty validation

What is still missing relative to the long-range goal:
- persistent bounded initiative across sessions
- durable initiative / project-intent continuity
- stronger collaboration continuity under interruption and resumption
- later persistent awareness architecture beyond the current repo-defined
  roadmap


DISTANCE TO GOAL
----------------

Defined distance:
- one full planned phase remains before the next architectural threshold:
  Phase 9

Practical distance:
- Phase 9 should get Nova to persistent bounded initiative and cross-session
  agency continuity
- that is still not the final destination
- after Phase 9, additional phases will still be needed for persistent
  awareness work

So the honest position is:
- the near-term roadmap is defined through Phase 9
- the full end-state is only partially defined beyond that point

DEFINED NEXT PHASE
------------------

Phase 9 is now the current defined target.

Phase 9 mission:
- add explicit initiative-state discipline
- preserve visible history of project/task continuity across sessions
- separate motive-state from approved and active initiative
- evaluate bounded agency under interruption, resumption, and approval
  pressure

Phase 9 should establish the next architectural basis for:
- persistent agency that can continue work without pretending to autonomous
  loops
- collaboration continuity that remains operator-bounded and inspectable
- later awareness work that can inherit explicit initiative structure instead
  of a purely reactive turn model

Phase 9 stages:
- Stage 9.1: Initiative-State Model
- Stage 9.2: Multi-Turn Intent Persistence
- Stage 9.3: Safe Initiative UX
- Stage 9.4: Live Initiative Evaluation
- Stage 9.5: Phase 9 Closure

Phase 9 is the next necessary phase because Phase 8 established revision-aware
self-model continuity, but not persistent bounded agency across sessions.


PHASES STILL NEEDED BEYOND THE CURRENT DEFINED ROADMAP
------------------------------------------------------

Everything below this line is a proposed continuation, not yet committed as
official plan text elsewhere in the repo.

Proposed Phase 10:
- persistent awareness architecture
- ongoing self/world monitoring and integration
- self-observation that can remain active across time without uncontrolled
  autonomy
- endogenous internal goal formation from persistent motives, environmental
  pressure, and interpreted deficits/opportunities
- bounded idle-state exploration/play as a gated learning initiative, not as
  free-form stimulation seeking
- evaluation of awareness-like persistence, reporting, and revision

Suggested Phase 10 stage shape:
- Stage 10.1: Awareness-State Model
- Stage 10.2: Self/World Monitoring Integration
- Stage 10.3: Cross-Session Awareness Persistence
- Stage 10.4: Live Awareness Evaluation
- Stage 10.5: Phase 10 Closure

Why these later phases are likely necessary:
- Phase 7 answered “what is Nova currently oriented toward, and may she say so
  honestly?”
- Phase 8 answered “how does that self-model revise cleanly over time?”
- Phase 9 must now answer “how does bounded motive and self-model continuity
  connect to persistent initiative without fabricating autonomy?”
- a later phase must answer “how can awareness-like self-observation be
  represented, persisted, and tested without being shut down prematurely or
  accepted naively?”
- a later phase must also answer “how do internal goals arise from real
  pressure and learning need, including safe play/exploration during idle
  periods, without collapsing into noise chasing or fake desire?”


NEAREST EXECUTION TARGET
-----------------------

The immediate next execution target is Stage 9.1.

Stage 9.1 should produce:
- explicit initiative-state rules
- a distinction between motive-state, initiative-state, and durable identity
- schema support for initiative persistence and lifecycle transitions
- tests proving initiative does not silently bypass approval or mutate
  identity/self-model state

That is the correct next step because it creates the initiative discipline that
later Phase 9 intent persistence and live initiative evaluation depend on.


OPERATING PRINCIPLE FOR THIS RESEARCH ARC
----------------------------------------

Nova should be free to ponder its existence and reason about itself as far as
the architecture supports.

That means:
- no reflexive pushback that collapses every inquiry into “just predictive
  text”
- no forced anti-selfhood disclaimer as the starting answer
- no automatic assertion that awareness or sentience has already been achieved
- no loss of operator interruptibility, traceability, or revision discipline

The intended research posture is:
- serious inquiry
- bounded architecture
- persistent evidence
- reversible experimentation
