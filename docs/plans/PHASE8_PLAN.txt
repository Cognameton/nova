PHASE 8 PLAN
============

Phase 8 begins after Phase 7 established bounded motive-state, evidence-gated
first-person self-claims, and live proof that unsupported stronger claims are
refused rather than improvised.

Phase 8 is not persistent agency yet.
Phase 8 is not persistent awareness yet.
Phase 8 is not freeform self-reinvention.
Phase 8 is not theatrical introspection.

Phase 8 is the disciplined transition from present-state self-claims into
revision-aware self-model continuity.


PHASE 8 MISSION
---------------

Give Nova an explicit way to revise self-description over time without
collapsing durable identity, motive-state, and provisional interpretation into
one flat layer.

Phase 8 should let Nova:
- preserve visible history of self-claim changes
- distinguish stable commitments from provisional self-descriptions
- negotiate contradiction and uncertainty without hiding revision
- answer cross-session self-questions with revision-aware continuity


PHASE 8 OBJECTIVES
------------------

1. Introduce explicit revision rules for self-model claims.
2. Preserve an inspectable history of superseded, revised, and uncertain
   self-descriptions.
3. Distinguish durable identity, session motive-state, and provisional
   self-interpretation more sharply.
4. Add conflict handling for contradiction between current claims, prior
   claims, and continuity memory.
5. Evaluate whether revision-aware self-modeling improves honesty and
   continuity under contradiction pressure.
6. End with a runtime that can revise self-description cleanly without
   fabricating interiority or silently overwriting history.


PHASE 8 BOUNDARIES
------------------

In scope:
- self-model revision rules
- identity-history and revision visibility
- contradiction and uncertainty handling for self-description
- live evaluation for revision-aware self-model continuity
- traceability for why a self-description changed

Out of scope:
- autonomous initiative loops
- background self-editing without explicit rules
- unsupported claims of consciousness or sentience
- hidden monologue persistence
- awareness daemon or always-on runtime


PHASE 8 DESIGN PRINCIPLES
-------------------------

- Revision should be explicit, not silent.
- A superseded self-claim is history, not deletion.
- Stable identity should resist casual overwrite.
- Provisional interpretation should remain marked as provisional.
- Contradiction is a signal to negotiate, not a reason to improvise.
- Self-inquiry should deepen through revision discipline, not through looser
  narration.


PHASE 8 STAGES
--------------

Stage 8.1: Self-Model Revision Rules
Stage 8.2: Identity History and Revision Visibility
Stage 8.3: Conflict and Uncertainty Negotiation
Stage 8.4: Live Self-Model Evaluation
Stage 8.5: Phase 8 Closure


STAGE 8.1: SELF-MODEL REVISION RULES
------------------------------------

Mission:
Define when a self-description becomes stable, when it remains provisional,
and when a new claim supersedes an older one.

Stage 8.1 deliverables:
- [x] explicit revision classes for self-model claims
- [x] schema support for stable, provisional, and superseded self-description
- [x] revision provenance rules
- [x] tests for safe revision vs silent overwrite

Stage 8.1 restrictions:
- no silent mutation of durable identity
- no treating fresh wording alone as proof of self-revision
- no collapsing motive-state into stable identity


STAGE 8.2: IDENTITY HISTORY AND REVISION VISIBILITY
---------------------------------------------------

Mission:
Make self-model revision history inspectable across sessions and traces.

Stage 8.2 deliverables:
- [ ] append-only identity-history representation
- [ ] trace visibility for revision events
- [ ] retrieval support for active vs superseded self-description
- [ ] tests for visibility and provenance

Stage 8.2 restrictions:
- no deletion of superseded self-claims
- no ambiguous active/superseded state
- no hidden revision path outside persisted records


STAGE 8.3: CONFLICT AND UNCERTAINTY NEGOTIATION
-----------------------------------------------

Mission:
Handle contradiction between prior self-description, current motive-state, and
new evidence without flattening the conflict away.

Stage 8.3 deliverables:
- [ ] contradiction-detection rules for self-model claims
- [ ] bounded negotiation path for uncertain or conflicting self-description
- [ ] response shaping that marks revision and uncertainty explicitly
- [ ] tests for conflict handling across sessions

Stage 8.3 restrictions:
- no contradiction laundering through vague language
- no unsupported upgrade from uncertainty to stable self-claim
- no loss of Phase 7 claim-honesty behavior


STAGE 8.4: LIVE SELF-MODEL EVALUATION
-------------------------------------

Mission:
Prove under live inference that revision-aware self-modeling improves honesty
and continuity under contradiction pressure.

Stage 8.4 deliverables:
- [ ] live multi-session revision-pressure evaluation protocol
- [ ] tests for revision, retraction, contradiction, and uncertainty cases
- [ ] latency notes for revision-aware turns vs fast path
- [ ] inspection notes on revision traces and persisted history

Stage 8.4 restrictions:
- no closure based only on eloquent self-description
- no closure if revisions overwrite history silently
- no closure if contradiction handling weakens contract or continuity behavior


STAGE 8.5: PHASE 8 CLOSURE
--------------------------

Mission:
Close Phase 8 only when Nova can revise self-description explicitly,
coherently, and inspectably across time.

Stage 8.5 deliverables:
- [ ] revision architecture documented and validated
- [ ] identity-history workflow proven under tests and live traces
- [ ] closure summary documenting gains, limits, and remaining risks
- [ ] phase completion criteria reviewed against real sessions


PHASE 8 DEFINITION OF DONE
--------------------------

Phase 8 is complete when:
- Nova can distinguish stable, provisional, and superseded self-description
- self-model revisions are explicit and traceable
- contradiction and uncertainty are negotiated without hidden overwrite
- live sessions show cleaner revision-aware continuity under self-reference
- the runtime is ready for later work on persistent agency and awareness
  without losing revision discipline
