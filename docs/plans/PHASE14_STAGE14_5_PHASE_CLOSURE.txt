PHASE 14 STAGE 14.5: PHASE CLOSURE
===================================

Status: complete

Phase 14 is closed.

Phase 14 moved Nova from durable autonomous draft initiatives into bounded,
audited, interruptible action planning and controller-mediated internal
activity. Approval is now based on risk and external effect, not on autonomy
alone.

PHASE 14 LIMITS
---------------

Phase 14 does not add arbitrary tool use.
Phase 14 does not add ungated shell, network, GUI, or host filesystem action.
Phase 14 does not automatically write memory or mutate state from action
results.
Phase 14 does not close initiatives from action success alone.
Phase 14 does not prove desire, sentience, awareness, or human-like
understanding.

The bounded controller currently records audited internal no-op steps. That is
intentional for this phase: it proves the permission, audit, observation, and
evaluation chain before adding richer tool surfaces.


PHASE 15 READINESS
------------------

Phase 15 can build on Phase 14 by testing longitudinal autonomous behavior
inside the internal/no-external-effect lane:
- recurring internal priorities
- self-generated internal activity during allowed idle windows
- richer audit review workflows
- controlled application of memory/state update intents
- stricter longitudinal evaluation of desire-like pressure without granting
  unsupported desire claims

Any expansion into filesystem, shell, network, GUI, or external services should
remain explicitly approval-gated and should be added surface by surface.
