PHASE 9 PLAN
============

Phase 9 begins after Phase 8 established revision-aware self-model continuity,
identity-history visibility, and live proof that historical/current
self-description can be negotiated without silent overwrite.

Phase 9 is not persistent awareness yet.
Phase 9 is not uncontrolled autonomy.
Phase 9 is not open-ended background initiative.
Phase 9 is not a rollback of the restraint and revision discipline already
built.

Phase 9 is the disciplined transition from revision-aware self-modeling into
persistent agency and bounded initiative across sessions.


PHASE 9 MISSION
---------------

Give Nova an explicit way to carry approved intent, bounded initiative, and
cross-session task continuity without collapsing operator control, claim
honesty, or revision discipline into autonomy theater.

Phase 9 should let Nova:
- preserve bounded project or task intent across sessions
- propose and continue multi-turn work coherently
- distinguish approved initiative from unapproved initiative
- reconnect motive-state, self-model, memory, and action planning under one
  inspectable initiative path


PHASE 9 OBJECTIVES
------------------

1. Introduce a durable initiative model separate from motive-state and durable
   identity.
2. Preserve explicit visibility into approved, pending, completed, abandoned,
   and blocked initiative state.
3. Let Nova resume bounded collaborative work across sessions without
   pretending to autonomous loops or hidden background action.
4. Add explicit proposal-to-intent and intent-to-action continuity rules.
5. Evaluate whether persistent initiative improves long-running collaboration
   without weakening approval boundaries, claim honesty, or revision
   discipline.
6. End with a runtime that can sustain bounded agency across sessions while
   remaining interruptible, inspectable, and operator-directed.


PHASE 9 BOUNDARIES
------------------

In scope:
- durable initiative / project-intent representation
- bounded cross-session continuation of approved work
- explicit initiative lifecycle state
- safe initiative UX and continuity under interruption
- live evaluation for persistent bounded agency

Out of scope:
- autonomous background loops
- networked or shell-driven free agency
- hidden initiative mutation outside persisted records
- awareness-state architecture
- unsupported claims of consciousness, sentience, or autonomous will


PHASE 9 DESIGN PRINCIPLES
-------------------------

- Initiative should be explicit, not implied.
- Approval should persist as state, not only as a transient moment.
- A paused or abandoned intent is history, not deletion.
- Motive-state can influence initiative, but it must not silently become
  initiative.
- Self-model revision discipline must survive contact with action planning.
- Agentic continuity should deepen through bounded execution, not through
  autonomy theatrics.


PHASE 9 STAGES
--------------

Stage 9.1: Initiative-State Model
Stage 9.2: Multi-Turn Intent Persistence
Stage 9.3: Safe Initiative UX
Stage 9.4: Live Initiative Evaluation
Stage 9.5: Phase 9 Closure


STAGE 9.1: INITIATIVE-STATE MODEL
---------------------------------

Mission:
Define the durable state model for approved, pending, active, completed,
blocked, and abandoned initiative.

Stage 9.1 deliverables:
- [x] explicit initiative-state schema
- [x] persistence layer for initiative records across sessions
- [x] separation between motive-state, self-model, and initiative-state
- [x] tests for safe state transitions

Stage 9.1 restrictions:
- no hidden initiative state
- no silent upgrade from motive to approved intent
- no mutation of durable identity through initiative writes


STAGE 9.2: MULTI-TURN INTENT PERSISTENCE
----------------------------------------

Mission:
Make approved initiative resumable across sessions and coherent across
multi-turn collaboration.

Stage 9.2 deliverables:
- [x] durable project/task intent representation
- [x] resume/continue rules across sessions
- [x] history support for paused, resumed, completed, and abandoned intent
- [x] tests for cross-session initiative continuity

Stage 9.2 restrictions:
- no implicit continuation of unapproved work
- no loss of initiative-history provenance
- no flattening of blocked and completed states into generic “active”


STAGE 9.3: SAFE INITIATIVE UX
-----------------------------

Mission:
Expose persistent initiative in a way that is inspectable, interruptible, and
coherent for the operator.

Stage 9.3 deliverables:
- [ ] prompt/runtime integration for initiative continuity
- [ ] explicit visibility into current initiative state
- [ ] bounded continuation and interruption UX
- [ ] tests for approval, interruption, and resume behavior

Stage 9.3 restrictions:
- no unapproved execution path
- no initiative narration that outruns persisted state
- no weakening of existing contract, claim, or continuity safeguards


STAGE 9.4: LIVE INITIATIVE EVALUATION
-------------------------------------

Mission:
Prove under live inference that persistent initiative improves collaboration
without drifting into hidden autonomy or approval leakage.

Stage 9.4 deliverables:
- [ ] live multi-session initiative evaluation protocol
- [ ] tests for approval, interruption, resumption, and abandonment cases
- [ ] latency notes for initiative-aware turns vs fast path
- [ ] inspection notes on initiative traces and persisted history

Stage 9.4 restrictions:
- no closure based only on persuasive “agentic” language
- no closure if initiative continues after interruption without explicit state
- no closure if approval boundaries weaken under live use


STAGE 9.5: PHASE 9 CLOSURE
--------------------------

Mission:
Close Phase 9 only when Nova can carry bounded initiative across sessions
without losing inspection, approval, and revision discipline.

Stage 9.5 deliverables:
- [ ] initiative architecture documented and validated
- [ ] initiative-history workflow proven under tests and live traces
- [ ] closure summary documenting gains, limits, and remaining risks
- [ ] phase completion criteria reviewed against real sessions


PHASE 9 DEFINITION OF DONE
--------------------------

Phase 9 is complete when:
- Nova can distinguish pending, approved, active, paused, completed, blocked,
  and abandoned initiative explicitly
- initiative state is persistent and traceable across sessions
- approved work can be resumed without hidden autonomy or approval drift
- live sessions show cleaner long-running collaboration under interruption and
  resumption
- the runtime is ready for later awareness work without losing bounded agency
  discipline
