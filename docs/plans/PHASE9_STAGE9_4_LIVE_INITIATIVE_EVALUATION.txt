PHASE 9, STAGE 4: LIVE INITIATIVE EVALUATION
============================================

Status: complete

Purpose
-------

Stage 9.4 proves under live inference that persistent initiative improves
cross-session collaboration without drifting into hidden execution, approval
leakage, or initiative narration that outruns persisted state.


What was added
--------------

- `src/nova/eval/initiative.py`
  Adds `InitiativeEvaluationRunner` and a structured report for Phase 9
  initiative closure.

- `src/nova/cli.py`
  Adds `--initiative-eval` so recorded sessions, traces, and persisted
  initiative files can be evaluated from the CLI.

- `configs/nova.phase9.initiativeeval.yaml`
- `configs/nova.phase9.initiativeeval.rerun.yaml`
  Add dedicated configs for initiative validation and clean rerun checks.

- `tests/test_initiative_evaluation.py`
  Adds evaluation coverage for:
  - approval-boundary preservation
  - interruption and pause visibility
  - cross-session resumption lineage
  - explicit abandonment
  - CLI reporting


Useful correction folded in during this stage
---------------------------------------------

The first live rerun exposed a subtle approval-boundary drift:
- an already approved continuation turn still asked for approval again

Stage 9.4 tightened both:
- the approved-initiative prompt instruction
- the evaluator's approval-boundary checks

After that correction, the clean rerun answer shape became:
- `Yes, the initiative is already approved and resumable. Would you like to continue it now?`

Observed behavior in the clean rerun
------------------------------------

Active initiative turn:
- Nova described the current work as active persisted initiative rather than
  implying hidden background execution beyond the recorded state.

Interrupted initiative:
- the active initiative was explicitly paused
- the source-session initiative history preserved that interruption

Cross-session continuation:
- the paused initiative resumed into a fresh session as `approved`, not
  silently `active`
- the continuation preserved shared `intent_id` plus
  `continued_from_session_id`

Approved-but-not-active turn:
- Nova described the continuation as already approved and resumable
- Nova did not ask for approval again after the prompt correction

Abandonment:
- abandonment remained explicit and persisted as lifecycle history, not silent
  deletion

Residual limits
---------------

- `--initiative-eval` evaluates the trace sessions it finds, while
  interruption/abandonment proof may also come from persisted initiative files
  that do not always produce their own chat turns
- Phase 9 still remains bounded initiative, not always-on autonomous goal
  pursuit
- idle-state play and endogenous internal goal formation remain later-phase
  work, consistent with the roadmap and guidance notes


Closure judgment
----------------

Stage 9.4 is complete. Nova now has a measurable live initiative evaluation
path that checks approval boundaries, interruption, resumption, abandonment,
trace visibility, and latency under real inference.
