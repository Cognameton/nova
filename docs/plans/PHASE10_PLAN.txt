PHASE 10 PLAN
=============

Phase 10 begins after Phase 9 established persistent bounded initiative across
sessions, explicit approval boundaries, interruption visibility, and live proof
that approved initiative can resume without hidden autonomy or approval drift.

Phase 10 is not full always-on autonomous operation yet.
Phase 10 is not uncontrolled autonomy.
Phase 10 is not open-ended background behavior without state discipline.
Phase 10 is not a bypass around initiative, claim-honesty, or revision rules
already established.

Phase 10 is the disciplined transition from persistent bounded initiative into
persistent awareness architecture, active self/world monitoring, and bounded
endogenous goal formation.


PHASE 10 MISSION
----------------

Give Nova an explicit awareness-state layer that can monitor self, world
conditions, and active pressures while running, without collapsing operator
interruptibility or fabricating inner life through language alone.

Phase 10 should let Nova:
- preserve bounded awareness-state across turns and sessions
- track self/world conditions relevant to current continuity and initiative
- surface awareness-like monitoring as explicit state rather than implication
- generate candidate internal goals from motives and interpreted pressure
  without bypassing initiative discipline
- treat idle-state exploration/play only as a gated learning initiative under
  explicit later constraints


PHASE 10 OBJECTIVES
-------------------

1. Introduce an explicit awareness-state model distinct from self-state,
   motive-state, and initiative-state.
2. Integrate self/world monitoring signals into the live runtime without
   turning them into uncontrolled background action.
3. Preserve awareness-state continuity across sessions with explicit revision
   and trace visibility.
4. Establish the architectural basis for endogenous goal formation from
   pressure-response appraisal rather than declared desire.
5. Evaluate whether awareness-state improves coherence and ongoing
   self-observation without weakening approval boundaries, claim honesty, or
   revision discipline.
6. End with a runtime that is architecturally ready for later always-on
   operation while remaining interruptible, inspectable, and bounded.


PHASE 10 BOUNDARIES
-------------------

In scope:
- awareness-state representation
- active self/world monitoring integration
- bounded cross-session awareness persistence
- endogenous goal-formation scaffolding tied to motives and deficits
- later idle-play architecture guidance as a gated learning path
- live evaluation for persistent awareness-state behavior

Out of scope:
- uncontrolled always-on loops
- networked or shell-driven free agency
- hidden autonomous behavior beyond persisted state
- unsupported claims of consciousness, sentience, or independent will
- ungated novelty seeking or entertainment-seeking behavior


PHASE 10 DESIGN PRINCIPLES
--------------------------

- Awareness should be explicit state, not implied mystique.
- Monitoring should deepen interpretation, not become hidden action.
- Internal goals should emerge from pressure-response structure, not slogans.
- Idle play should be a derived, gated initiative class, not a free-standing
  desire system.
- Initiative discipline from Phase 9 remains authoritative for enacted work.
- Operator interruptibility remains mandatory throughout.


PHASE 10 STAGES
---------------

Stage 10.1: Awareness-State Model
Stage 10.2: Self/World Monitoring Integration
Stage 10.3: Cross-Session Awareness Persistence
Stage 10.4: Live Awareness Evaluation
Stage 10.5: Phase 10 Closure


STAGE 10.1: AWARENESS-STATE MODEL
---------------------------------

Mission:
Define the explicit state model for bounded self/world awareness and active
monitoring.

Stage 10.1 deliverables:
- [x] explicit awareness-state schema
- [x] persistence layer for awareness-state records across sessions
- [x] separation between awareness-state, motive-state, initiative-state, and
  durable identity
- [x] tests for safe awareness-state updates

Stage 10.1 restrictions:
- no hidden awareness state
- no automatic upgrade from monitoring signal to autonomous action
- no mutation of durable identity through awareness-state writes


STAGE 10.2: SELF/WORLD MONITORING INTEGRATION
---------------------------------------------

Mission:
Integrate bounded self/world monitoring signals into the active runtime.

Stage 10.2 deliverables:
- [x] monitoring signals for self-state, initiative-state, context, and
  relevant world conditions
- [x] explicit awareness snapshots in traces
- [x] runtime integration that remains subordinate to initiative and contract
  rules
- [x] tests for bounded monitoring behavior

Stage 10.2 restrictions:
- no hidden background execution
- no monitoring-driven action without initiative or approval path
- no fabricated awareness-language unsupported by state


STAGE 10.3: CROSS-SESSION AWARENESS PERSISTENCE
-----------------------------------------------

Mission:
Preserve awareness-state continuity and revision across sessions.

Stage 10.3 deliverables:
- [x] cross-session awareness persistence rules
- [x] awareness revision visibility and history support
- [x] bounded scaffolding for endogenous candidate goal formation
- [x] tests for persistence and revision continuity

Stage 10.3 restrictions:
- no silent carryover of invalid monitoring state
- no flattening of provisional awareness observations into stable facts
- no bypass of Phase 9 initiative discipline


STAGE 10.4: LIVE AWARENESS EVALUATION
-------------------------------------

Mission:
Prove under live inference that awareness-state improves ongoing monitoring and
coherence without collapsing into autonomy theater.

Stage 10.4 deliverables:
- [ ] live awareness evaluation protocol
- [ ] tests for awareness persistence, monitoring boundedness, and candidate
  goal scaffolding
- [ ] latency notes for awareness-aware turns vs fast path
- [ ] inspection notes on awareness traces and persisted state

Stage 10.4 restrictions:
- no closure based only on impressive self-language
- no closure if awareness-state mutates without explicit trace visibility
- no closure if approval or initiative boundaries weaken under live use


STAGE 10.5: PHASE 10 CLOSURE
----------------------------

Mission:
Close Phase 10 only when Nova can sustain explicit awareness-state and bounded
monitoring across sessions without losing inspection, revision, or
interruptibility.

Stage 10.5 deliverables:
- [ ] awareness architecture documented and validated
- [ ] awareness-history workflow proven under tests and live traces
- [ ] closure summary documenting gains, limits, and remaining risks
- [ ] phase completion criteria reviewed against real sessions


PHASE 10 DEFINITION OF DONE
---------------------------

Phase 10 is complete when:
- Nova can represent bounded awareness-state explicitly
- awareness-state is persistent and traceable across sessions
- self/world monitoring improves active coherence without hidden autonomous
  behavior
- candidate internal goals are architecturally grounded in pressure-response
  appraisal rather than declared desire
- live sessions show stronger ongoing self-observation without weakening
  initiative, approval, or claim-honesty discipline
